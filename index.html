Community Service Project

This as a voluntary initiative undertaken by individuals or groups to address a specific need or issue within a community. It involves dedicating time, skills, and resources to benefit others and make a positive impact on the community.    

It desires to create positive change,and improve the well-being of individuals and the overall community.
